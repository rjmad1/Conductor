## Primary Segment: Digital-Native SMBs

**Definition:** Small and medium businesses with 5–500 employees that already use WhatsApp as a primary customer communication channel and operate with limited IT resources.

### Qualifying Criteria
- Uses WhatsApp Business for customer communication
- Has at least one customer-facing function (sales, support, appointments, delivery)
- Does NOT have a dedicated IT team or developer on staff
- Monthly revenue: ₹5L–₹5Cr (indicative)
- Willing to pay ₹1,500–₹15,000/month for automation tools

---

## Target Verticals

### 1. Healthcare & Wellness

**Profile:** Clinics, hospitals, diagnostic labs, physiotherapy centers, dental practices, wellness centers

**Core pain:** Manual appointment management, prescription reminders, lab report delivery, patient follow-up

**Key automations needed:**
- Doctor appointment booking and reminders
- Prescription refill reminders
- Lab report delivery via WhatsApp
- Vaccination reminders
- Post-consultation follow-up workflows
- Patient feedback collection

**Why prioritize:** Immediate, measurable ROI (no-show reduction is directly quantifiable). Low purchase cycle hesitation (compliance-conscious → will pay for documented systems).

**Decision maker:** Clinic owner / Practice manager  
**Budget authority:** Owner or administrator  
**Typical plan:** Growth (₹4,999/month)

---

### 2. Retail & E-Commerce

**Profile:** Online stores (Shopify, WooCommerce), D2C brands, multi-brand retail, boutiques

**Core pain:** Abandoned cart recovery, order updates, returns management, repeat purchase campaigns

**Key automations needed:**
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Order confirmation, shipping, delivery updates
- Return/refund management
- Restock alerts, wishlist notifications
- Loyalty campaigns, birthday/anniversary offers
- COD verification
- Product catalog sharing

**Why prioritize:** Shopify/WooCommerce connectors are standard → immediate integration value. High volume = high overage revenue.

**Decision maker:** Founder / E-commerce manager  
**Budget authority:** Founder or marketing lead  
**Typical plan:** Growth → Business (₹4,999–₹12,999/month)

---

### 3. Professional Services

**Profile:** CA firms, law firms, consulting firms, recruitment agencies, marketing agencies, financial advisors

**Core pain:** Lead management, client follow-up, appointment scheduling, document collection, payment collection

**Key automations needed:**
- Lead capture and qualification
- Appointment booking and scheduling
- Proposal delivery automation
- Client follow-up sequences
- Document collection workflows
- Payment reminders and collection
- Referral campaign management

**Why prioritize:** High LTV (relationship-based business → sticky customers). High ARPC potential (business plan). Lower message volumes but higher workflow complexity.

**Decision maker:** Managing partner / Business development head  
**Budget authority:** Partner / Director  
**Typical plan:** Business (₹12,999/month)

---

### 4. Education

**Profile:** Coaching institutes, K-12 schools, EdTech platforms, skill development centers, universities

**Core pain:** Admissions, fee collection, student/parent communication, attendance, assignment reminders

**Key automations needed:**
- Admissions inquiry handling
- Fee reminder and collection
- Student communication workflows
- Parent engagement and updates
- Assignment and exam reminders
- Course enrollment workflows
- Performance report delivery

**Why prioritize:** Recurring annual revenue (fee seasons). Large contact base (parents = marketing channel). Low tech sophistication → high value of zero-code platform.

**Decision maker:** Director / Principal / Admissions head  
**Budget authority:** Owner or management  
**Typical plan:** Growth → Business

---

### 5. Real Estate

**Profile:** Real estate brokerages, builders, property consultants, co-working spaces

**Core pain:** Lead qualification, site visit scheduling, property updates, broker communication, payment follow-up

**Key automations needed:**
- Lead qualification conversations
- Site visit scheduling and reminders
- Property brochure delivery
- Post-visit follow-up
- Payment milestone notifications
- Broker communication automation
- New project launch campaigns

**Why prioritize:** High deal values → high perceived ROI of even 1 additional conversion. Brokers are heavy WhatsApp users.

**Decision maker:** Sales head / Founder  
**Budget authority:** Director / Owner  
**Typical plan:** Business (₹12,999/month)

| Healthcare | Very High | High | Low | Very High | **P1** |
| Professional Services | High | High | Medium | High | **P1** |
| Retail/E-Commerce | High | Medium | Medium-High | High | **P2** |

## Customer Acquisition Segments

### Segment A: Self-Serve (Digital-native, <50 employees)
- Discovery: Google search, product review sites, WhatsApp business communities
- Purchase: Website trial → self-serve upgrade
- Onboarding: In-product, templated
- Target plan: Starter → Growth

### Segment B: Assisted (Growth-phase, 50-200 employees)
- Discovery: Partner referral, case studies, industry events
- Purchase: Demo → proposal → assisted onboarding
- Onboarding: CSM-led, 2-3 session setup
- Target plan: Growth → Business

### Segment C: Enterprise (200+ employees, multi-location)
- Discovery: Direct outreach, SI partner, industry reference
- Purchase: Evaluation → PoC → contract
- Onboarding: Dedicated implementation support
- Target plan: Enterprise (custom)
